Max Temperature vs. City Latitude

As expected, a trend is shown where the closer a city is to the equator (Lat = 0), the higher the max temperature. As you move away from the equator, the max temperature gradually decreases with cities above the equator showing a marginally larger decrease.

You can see a trend where the countries South of the equator have a higher average temperature than the countries North of the equator. However, if you account for the fact that there are more North (positive latitude) with a greater distance from the equator than South, this comparison becomes less obvious and would need to be looked into further.
